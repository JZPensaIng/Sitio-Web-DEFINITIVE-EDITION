Codex: United Colonies

The United Colonies (UC) is the most powerful, established military and political faction in the Settled Systems. Based in New Atlantis on the planet Jemison, the UC embodies the spirit of a centralized, law-abiding republic.

They maintain a large, disciplined fleet and control vast territories. While seen as a beacon of stability by some, others view their expansion and bureaucracy with suspicion. Relations with the Freestar Collective remain tense after the Colony War.
