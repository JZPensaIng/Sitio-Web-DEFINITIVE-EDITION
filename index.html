Codex: United Colonies

The United Colonies (UC) is the most powerful, established military and political faction in the Settled Systems. Based in New Atlantis on the planet Jemison, the UC embodies the spirit of a centralized, law-abiding republic.

They maintain a large, disciplined fleet and control vast territories. While seen as a beacon of stability by some, others view their expansion and bureaucracy with suspicion. Relations with the Freestar Collective remain tense after the Colony War, but a fragile peace currently holds.

The UC Citizens are proud and loyal, benefiting from advanced technology and social stability. However, the rigid structure can feel oppressive to those who value personal freedom above all else, often leading them to the FreAR Collective.

Their military prowess is unmatched, with highly trained soldiers and state-of-the-art spacecraft. Recruitment into the UC Navy or Vanguard is a prestigious path, offering a secure career and a chance to defend the core worlds from external threats.

Research and development are heavily funded by the UC, leading to breakthroughs in grav-drive technology, robotics, and terraforming. These advancements solidify their position as the technological leader in the Settled Systems.

New Atlantis, the capital, is a sprawling metropolis of towering skyscrapers, verdant parks, and bustling marketplaces. It serves as a hub for trade, diplomacy, and interspecies cooperation, a true testament to human ambition among the stars.
